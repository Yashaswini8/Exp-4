# Exp - 4 HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

# AIM:
To design a system that efficiently manages hospital operations such as patient records, doctor scheduling, billing, and inventory.

# SRS (Procedure):

1. Register patients and maintain their medical history.
2. Manage doctor details, appointments, and duty schedules.
3. Handle inpatient and outpatient records systematically.
4. Generate prescriptions and store diagnostic reports.
5. Manage billing, payments, and insurance details.
6. Maintain pharmacy and inventory stock records.
7. Provide administrative reports and ensure data security.
